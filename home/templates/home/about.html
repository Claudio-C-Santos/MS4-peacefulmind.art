About

Tiago Barata started his journey back in 2012 when he decided to go on a backpacking trip to India. His goal was to discover more about the world and about himself. This first trip was 2 years long with a lot of ups and downs but at the end everything was a lesson for him. After coming back, not feeling that he had taken enough from his first trip, he decided to go back to India. This second trip provided different experiences as he was already comfortable with the ins and outs of living in India. He started learning how to make craft jewelry out of the available materials around him. It all start as a way to spend time and to keep his mind challenged during the covid pandemic but it quickly became more than that. Has he, and the people around him, noticed that his skills were improving everyday he decided to take it serious. Made several trips around India searching for the best and most beautiful stones he could find and met smiths who taught him how to works certain materials like bronze.
